The journey

Journey Towards Success

The oversight of a cooperative housing society's requirements falls under the purview of society management. Society management encompasses various crucial tasks, such as generating maintenance invoices and receipts, collecting maintenance fees, accounting for community funds, managing helpdesk operations, facilitating facility management, overseeing staff management, and ensuring effective gate security management.

Behind the story

How Our Smart Housing Started

In a housing society, there exists a multitude of requirements ranging from maintenance collection and facility bookings to seamless communication between residents and managing committees. We emerges as the all-inclusive platform that comprehensively caters to these diverse society needs, offering an integrated solution that simplifies and streamlines every aspect of management.
