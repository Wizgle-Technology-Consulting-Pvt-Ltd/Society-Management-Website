Our Story

The story behind Our Smart Housing

On a relentless pursuit to find convenient and tech-enhanced solutions that simplify and optimize the management of your housing facilities.

The journey

Journey Towards Success

The oversight of a housing society's needs and requirements falls under the purview of society management. Creating maintenance invoices and receipts, collecting maintenance fees, accounting for community funds, managing helpdesk operations, facilitating facility management, overseeing staff management, and ensuring effective gate security management are all part of routine tasks covered under society management. Streamlining and automating these day-to-day society operations is at the core of our mission statement so as to give the residents a comfortable, smooth, and e-smart housing experience.

Behind the story

How OSH Started

In a housing society, there exists several requirements that must be met, ranging from collecting maintenance to scheduling facilities to ensuring a smooth and seamless communication between the residents and managing committees. We position ourselves as an all-inclusive platform that caters to all of society’s diverse needs, offering an integrated solution that streamlines and simplifies every aspect of housing management.
